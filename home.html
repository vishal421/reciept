Is this fuel bill generator completely free? +
Yes, FuelBill.in is 100% free to use. You can generate, preview and download as many petrol or diesel receipts as you need — no account, no subscription, no hidden charges.
Can I download the petrol bill as a PDF? +
Bills are downloaded as high-resolution PNG images. You can easily convert them to PDF using any free online tool, or directly print the PNG from your browser using File → Print and saving as PDF.
Is the generated fuel bill valid for office reimbursement? +
This tool is designed for educational, training and personal record-keeping purposes. For official employer reimbursements, we recommend using actual receipts from the petrol pump. Always follow your company's expense policy.
Which petrol pump logos are available? +
Currently Indian Oil (IOC), Hindustan Petroleum (HP) and Bharat Petroleum (BPCL) logos are included. These cover the three major public sector oil marketing companies in India.
Can I add my GSTIN or VAT number? +
Yes. The Optional Fields section lets you toggle GSTIN, CST No, LST No, and VAT No on or off. When enabled, you can enter the corresponding values in the data fields below.
Does the fuel bill generator work on mobile phones? +
Yes, the generator is fully responsive and works on all smartphones and tablets. You can create and download a petrol receipt directly from your phone without needing a desktop.
Is there a watermark on downloaded receipts? +
No watermark is added to your downloaded receipt. The output is a clean, realistic thermal-style receipt image without any branding from FuelBill.in overlaid on it.

What is a Fuel Bill Generator?

A fuel bill generator is a free online tool that creates realistic petrol pump receipts and diesel bills in seconds. Whether you need a petrol bill for office reimbursement, a diesel receipt for vehicle expense claims, or simply want to generate a PoS-style thermal receipt for record-keeping, FuelBill.in delivers a professional output instantly — with no registration.

How to Generate a Fuel Bill Online

Using our online petrol receipt generator is straightforward. Select your preferred template (thermal or card payment), choose a pump logo, optionally enable GST fields, then fill in the fuel details like amount, volume, rate, date and vehicle number. The live preview updates as you type — hit Download Receipt when ready.

Fuel Bill for Reimbursement

Many companies in India require employees to submit a fuel bill for reimbursement. Our generator creates receipts in the standard Indian petrol pump format — with receipt number, FIP number, nozzle number, rate per litre, volume dispensed, total amount, and date. The output closely matches what real PoS terminals print.

Indian Oil, HP & Bharat Petroleum Templates

As the three largest fuel retailers in India, Indian Oil (IOC), Hindustan Petroleum (HP) and Bharat Petroleum (BPCL) templates are all supported. Simply select your logo from the panel and the receipt will display the corresponding company branding in a greyscale thermal-print style.

Diesel Bill Generator

The tool works equally well as a diesel bill generator. Just change the Product field from "Petrol" to "Diesel" (or "HSD" for High Speed Diesel) and update the rate per litre accordingly. All other fields remain the same.
