What is a Fuel Bill Generator?

A fuel bill generator is a free online tool that lets you instantly create realistic petrol and diesel receipts. Whether you need a petrol bill for office reimbursement, a diesel receipt for vehicle expense claims, or simply want to generate a PoS-style thermal receipt, FuelBill.in gives you a professional output in seconds — no registration needed.

How to Generate a Fuel Bill Online

  1. Choose your preferred template — Thermal Receipt or Card Payment style
  2. Select a paper texture for a realistic look
  3. Pick your pump logo — Indian Oil, HP, or Bharat Petroleum
  4. Enable optional fields like GSTIN, CST, LST, VAT as needed
  5. Fill in your fuel details — amount, volume, rate, date, vehicle number
  6. Click Download Receipt to save as a high-quality PNG image
