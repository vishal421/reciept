FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our free bill generators.

Can I use this fuel bill for office reimbursement? +
Yes. FuelBill.in generates petrol and diesel bills in the standard Indian petrol pump format — with receipt number, FIP number, nozzle number, rate per litre, volume dispensed, vehicle number and total amount. These are the exact fields required for office fuel reimbursement submissions to HR and accounts departments.
What is a petrol pump bill book and how does this help? +
A petrol pump bill book is a record of fuel purchase receipts. FuelBill.in is your digital petrol pump bill book — re-create any petrol or diesel receipt with correct pump name, receipt number, FIP number, and transaction details, in the standard format accepted by Indian employers for conveyance and travel reimbursement.
Is this bill generator completely free? +
Yes, FuelBill.in is 100% free. Generate, preview and download petrol, diesel or food bills — no account, no subscription, no charges ever.
Can I generate both fuel bills and food bills? +
Yes! FuelBill.in has two separate tools — the Fuel Bill Generator for petrol & diesel receipts, and the Food Bill Generator for restaurant bills with CGST & SGST auto-calculation.
Does the food bill include GST? +
Yes. CGST 2.5% and SGST 2.5% are automatically calculated on the taxable amount. You can also toggle an optional 10% service charge on or off.
Which petrol pump logos are available? +
Indian Oil (IOC), Hindustan Petroleum (HP) and Bharat Petroleum (BPCL) logos are included — the three major public sector oil marketing companies in India.
Can I download as PDF? +
Bills are downloaded as high-resolution PNG images. You can easily convert to PDF using any free tool, or print directly from your browser (File → Print → Save as PDF) — ideal for attaching to expense reimbursement forms.
Does it work on mobile phones? +
Yes, both generators are fully responsive and work on all smartphones and tablets. Create and download bills directly from your phone.
Is there a watermark on downloaded bills? +
No watermark is added. The output is a clean, realistic receipt image with no branding from FuelBill.in overlaid on it.
