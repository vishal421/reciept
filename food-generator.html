Restaurant Bill with CGST & SGST

Indian restaurant bills include CGST (Central GST) and SGST (State GST) at 2.5% each on the taxable amount. Our generator calculates these automatically — no manual calculation needed. The grand total is rounded off and displayed on the receipt exactly as a real restaurant bill would show.
