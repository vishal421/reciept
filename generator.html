Frequently Asked Questions — Petrol Bill Generator

Is this petrol bill generator completely free?

Yes, 100% free. No signup, no subscription, no watermark. Download as many petrol or diesel bills as you need at no cost.

Which oil company logos are available?

Indian Oil (IOCL), Hindustan Petroleum (HP/HPCL), and Bharat Petroleum (BPCL). Select your pump brand in Step 3 — the correct header, logo, and footer text will appear automatically.

Can I generate a diesel bill (HSD) using this tool?

Yes. In the Enter Details section, set the product to Diesel or HSD. All templates support both petrol (MS) and diesel (HSD) bill generation.

How do I get a petrol bill PDF?

Click the green PDF button in the action bar. The petrol bill PDF will be generated and downloaded directly. You can also download the PNG and use File → Print → Save as PDF in any browser.

What is the petrol bill used for?

This tool is intended for personal record keeping only — maintaining your own monthly fuel expense log, understanding the standard fuel bill format, fleet cost tracking, or educational reference. It must not be used for official reimbursement, tax deduction, or any legal purpose.

What does FIP number mean on a petrol bill?

FIP stands for Fuel Inlet Point — it identifies the specific fuel dispenser (machine) at the petrol pump. Combined with the Nozzle number, it records exactly which unit dispensed the fuel.
