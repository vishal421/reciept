What is a Petrol Bill Generator?

A petrol bill generator is an online tool that helps you create a petrol pump receipt in the standard Indian PoS (Point of Sale) format. It is used for personal record keeping when you need a copy of your petrol purchase with all the correct details — pump name, rate per litre, quantity, total amount, vehicle number, date and time.

FuelBill.in is India's free petrol bill generator. You get a realistic, well-formatted receipt that looks exactly like what you receive at an actual petrol pump. Choose from three major oil company logos — Indian Oil (IOC), Hindustan Petroleum (HP), or Bharat Petroleum (BPCL) — and customise every field.

Petrol Bill Format — What Fields Does It Include?

The standard petrol bill format in India includes the following fields that you can fill in on our generator:

  • Petrol pump name and address — the dealer name and location
  • Receipt number and Local ID — unique transaction identifiers
  • FIP number and Nozzle number — fuel island point details
  • Product — Petrol / MS (Motor Spirit)
  • Rate per litre — current petrol price in your city
  • Volume in litres — quantity dispensed
  • Total amount (₹) — the amount paid
  • Vehicle registration number
  • Date, time, and payment mode — Cash or Card

Why Do People Use a Petrol Receipt Generator?

There are many legitimate reasons to use a petrol receipt generator for personal purposes:

  • You lost the original receipt at the pump and need a personal copy for your own records
  • You want to maintain a monthly fuel expense log
  • You need to understand the standard petrol invoice format India for training or reference
  • You manage a small fleet and need to track individual vehicle fuel costs

Important: Bills generated on FuelBill.in are for personal record keeping and reference only. They are not official documents and should never be submitted for any legal, tax, or official reimbursement purpose.
